Adobe Illustrator Design

Application Used:
Adobe Illustrator

This package is designed for a module project and it's based on the theme of what your ideal business would be like if you had one. We engaged a set of requirements which includes:

- Name Card (front & back)
- Webpages (Home, About, Portfolio, Contact)
- Stickers (A set of five)

As I enjoy fashion a lot, I decided to create a theme related to fashion which shows my ideal business as a fashion designer. The business name card is designed with fashion elements in mind and stickers which are related to possible items which are sold in fashion retail stores.
